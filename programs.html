Programs



We have two programs that trainees could participate. One is a formal training and the other one is a self-paced personal learning.


Formal Training

We offer selected IT courses on tuition basis. Most courses last three months on the basis of 2-hour classes being offered twice a week on different days. Some courses take up to nine months to complete. There are different levels of training in the subjects - beginner, intermediate, and advanced levels.


Personal Learning

We consult those who are determined to learn IT personally because they do not have the opportunity to participate in formal IT trainings. There would be a minimal fee for the consultation.


Who the program is for

This program is for anyone tech lover who does not have the opportunity to pursue formal college studies in IT. Our program is particularly beneficial to people with the following background, in a descending order:

  • College graduates in IT, Math, Statistics, or Engineering, graduates who would like to add some specific additional IT skills or refresh those skills.
  • College graduates in any subjects who would like to jump-start the IT profession.
  • High school graduates and those who have some college study.

Some low wage workers such as taxicab drivers could benefit much from this program since they can invest their call waiting time in IT learning. Home stay moms (or dads) could similarly benefit from the program since they can turn their extra time as they attend their infants or kids.


Benefits of the Programs

Benefits from our programs range from enjoying a hobby, to sharpening of one’s mind, to job employment. An elderly trainee once said, “I would even consider this program to keep my mind sharp or make it sharper”. However, the extent to which a trainee or a learner can benefit varies depending on the individual trainee’s situation – e. g., education background, determination to learn, and amount of effort spent on learning. Our program would give a good knowledge and experience for those trainees who would want to attend other advanced trainings such as IT boot camps.
